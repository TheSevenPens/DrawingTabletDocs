## How I clean my tablets

First, I remove dust from tablets, pens, and monitors with a microfiber duster. Specifically this one: the **OXO Good Grips Microfiber Hand Duster** ($12) that I bought on Amazon.

Then, I wipe gently wipe down the tablet with a damp (not wet) cloth. If the tablet is a little greasy I may put a bit of soap with the damp cloth.&#x20;
